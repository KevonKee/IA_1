About me


My name is Kevon Kee and I was born on September 19th, 2002 in Kingston Jamaica. I'm a quiet person and I tend to keep to myself but I do talk alot around my closest friends and family. Some of my hobbies are watching Formula one, cycling, reading and playing video games

Likes and dislikes

My favorite color is blue and one of my favorite foods is cheeseburgers. I enjoy outdoor activities such as taking hikes and cycling. Some of my favorite holiday activites are ziplining, cliff diving and riding ATVs

Some things I dislike include the beach, ice cream and people who do not keep their word. The first 2 may seem strange but how much do you really like having sand all over your body? I also have sensitive teeth which makes eating ice cream quite unpleasant!

Inspirational person
